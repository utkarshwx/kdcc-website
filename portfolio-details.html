A Shared Vision for Digestive and Women's Health

Together, Dr. Bijay Misra and Dr. Lipsa Misra share a powerful vision for KDCC Health Care Private Limited: to create a center of excellence that significantly impacts the Gastro, Liver & Pancreas space and women's health. Their combined expertise, dedication to patient-centric care, and commitment to the latest medical advancements drive the clinic's mission to provide comprehensive, compassionate, and cutting-edge healthcare solutions to the community of Bhubaneswar and beyond.


Dr. Bijay Misra

M.B.B.S, M.D (Med). D.M (Gastro)
Consultant,
Gastroenterologist, Hepatologist & GI Endoscopist

Dr. Bijay Misra is the esteemed Director of KDCC, bringing a wealth of experience and expertise in the field of Gastroenterology, Hepatology, and GI Endoscopy. As a highly skilled Consultant, Dr. Bijay is dedicated to providing exceptional care to patients with a wide range of gastrointestinal, liver, and pancreatic disorders.

Driven by a passion for improving patient outcomes and advancing the understanding of digestive diseases, Dr. Misra's leadership is instrumental in shaping the vision and direction of KDCC Health Care Private Limited.


Dr. Lipsa Misra

MBBS, MD - Obstetrics & Gynaecology
Infertility Specialist

Dr. Lipsa Misra is a distinguished Director of KDCC, bringing her extensive experience and specialization in Obstetrics & Gynaecology and Infertility. With 19 years of overall experience, including 9 years as a specialist, Dr. Lipsa Misra is committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to women's health needs

Her expertise in infertility further strengthens the holistic approach of KDCC, recognizing the interconnectedness of overall well-being. Dr. Lipsa Misra's dedication to her patients and her profound understanding of women's health are invaluable assets to the clinic.
