Liver Services (Hepatology)

  • Management of Liver Disease

    • We provide comprehensive and personalized care for a wide spectrum of liver conditions, including fatty liver disease, autoimmune hepatitis, primary biliary cholangitis, and primary sclerosing cholangitis. Our approach focuses on accurate diagnosis, effective treatment strategies, and long-term management to optimize liver health.
  • Jaundice

    • Jaundice, characterized by the yellowing of the skin and eyes, is a symptom of underlying liver or bile duct issues. We offer thorough diagnostic evaluations to identify the cause of jaundice and provide appropriate medical or endoscopic management to address the underlying condition and alleviate symptoms.
  • Liver Cirrhosis

    • We offer expert management for all stages and complications of liver cirrhosis, a condition of advanced liver scarring. Our care focuses on slowing the progression of the disease, managing complications such as ascites and variceal bleeding, and evaluating patients for liver transplantation when appropriate.
  • Hepatitis

    • We provide comprehensive diagnosis, treatment, and management for all types of viral hepatitis (A, B, and C) as well as other forms of hepatitis. Our goal is to effectively manage the infection, prevent long-term liver damage, and improve the overall well-being of our patients.
  • Liver Cancer

    • We offer a multidisciplinary approach to the diagnosis, staging, and management of liver cancer. Our team collaborates to provide individualized treatment plans, which may include surgical resection, ablation therapies, chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and participation in clinical trials.
  • Ascites

    • Ascites, the accumulation of fluid in the abdomen, is a common complication of liver disease. We provide effective strategies for managing ascites, including dietary modifications, medications, and paracentesis (fluid drainage), to improve patient comfort and prevent complications.
  • Alcoholic Liver Disease

    • We offer specialized care for patients suffering from liver damage resulting from excessive alcohol consumption. Our approach includes medical management, lifestyle modifications, and support services to help patients achieve and maintain liver health.
  • FibroScan

    • This non-invasive and painless technology provides a quantitative assessment of liver stiffness and fibrosis (scarring). It is a valuable tool for the early detection and monitoring of various liver diseases, helping to guide treatment decisions without the need for a liver biopsy in many cases.
